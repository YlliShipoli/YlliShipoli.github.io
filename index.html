Exercises

Choose the muscle group you want to target.
Chest

Chest exercises are essential for developing and strengthening the muscles in the chest area.Effective chest exercises like bench press,push-ups,and dumbbell flyes build strength and definition,enhancing overall upper body fitness.

Upper Back

Upper back exercises are crucial for posture and strength.Incorporate rows,pull-ups,and lat pulldowns to target muscles like rhomboids and traps.

Lats

Lats exercises target the latissimus dorsi muscles for a V-shaped back.Include pull-ups,lat pulldowns and rows for effective development.

Shoulders

Shoulder exercises build deltoid muscles for strength and aesthetics.Include overhead press,lateral raises,and front raises in your routine.

Triceps

Triceps exercises are vital for arm strength and size.Effective options include dips,tricep extensions,and close-grip bench press.

Biceps

Biceps exercises enhance arm strength and appearance.Incorporate curls,chin-ups,and hammer curls for comprehensive biceps development in your routine

Legs

Leg exercises are crucial for overall strength and stability.Squats,lunges,and deadlifts target major muscle groups,promoting functional fitness.

Abs

Abs exercises are essential for core strength and definition.Include planks,crunches,and leg raises to target different abdominal muscles.
