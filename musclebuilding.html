Workout Description

Muscle-building workouts,also known as strength training or resistance training, are designed to stimulate muscle growth and increase overall strength. These workouts typically involve lifting weights or using resistance to challenge and overload the muscles,leading to adaptations such as increased muscle size,strength, and endurance.Here are some key principles and popular exercises commonly associated with muscle-building workouts:

Key Principles Of Muscle Building Workouts:

  • Progressive Overload
  • Compound Movements
  • Proper Form
  • Rest and Recovery
